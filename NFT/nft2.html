How Does It Work?

Weekly Giveaways

The weekly jackpot will be drawn every Sunday at 8 PM EST via Livestream held by our team members.

This will be done to ensure transparency between the developers and the community, as well as to eliminate the fear of fraudulent activity. Other measures will also be taken to ensure this as well.

A random number generator will pick a number between 1 and 5000

The wallet address that holds the winning number will be airdropped the weekly jackpot

Daily Giveaways

Daily sweepstakes will occur randomly throughout the day and will take place via airdrop as well

These will be smaller in size compared to the jackpot but will happen a lot more frequently

These drawings will not be live-streamed, but our team will let the community know when these are taking place.

Each winning address will be stored and restricted to one win per week. This is to ensure that we are spreading wealth and fairness to our community. Restrictions will reset every Sunday immediately following the weekly jackpot.
